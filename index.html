FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do I own the logo?

Yes — after final payment, you own the final logo files and can use them commercially.

What files do I get?

Depending on the package: PNG/JPG and usually SVG/PDF. Brand Kit includes a mini brand guide.

Can you match a style I like?

Yes. Share links to styles you like and we’ll use them as direction (not as a template).

What if I need more concepts?

You can add extra concepts as an add-on or upgrade your package anytime.
